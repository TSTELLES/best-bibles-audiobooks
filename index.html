📖 Welcome

Welcome everyone to the official page of Best Bibles Audiobooks, an independent project created by T. S. Telles, author of Christian-themed fiction and fantasy.

This initiative was born from a simple yet bold mission:

  • ✅ To challenge the overpriced audio Bibles on the market — which often limit access to God’s Word through high costs and restrictive distribution.
  • ✅ To reach people and countries where clear, high-quality Bibles are scarce or even censored.
  • ✅ To use any profit exclusively for expanding production and distribution, aiming to make these Bibles freely available on as many platforms as possible, and without ads.

By purchasing or streaming these Bibles, you directly help break barriers that keep many from hearing the Word of God in their own language, with clarity and dignity.

"Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature." - Mark 16:15

🌍 Why It Matters

Your support today fuels tomorrow’s freedom.

Every minute streamed, every Bible shared, every book read helps push this project closer to its ultimate goal: a world where no one is kept from the truth because of cost, censorship, or marketing agendas.

Thank you for being part of this mission. May these works bless you — and may you help them bless many more.

✍️ About the Adapter

T. S. Telles is not only dedicated to spreading the Bible in audio format — but is also the author of the novel Welcome to New York — Part 1, now available on Amazon.

This story offers a romance shaped by Christian values and daily faith, free from gratuitous nudity or profanity, focusing instead on characters and plots that nurture the reader’s spiritual and moral well-being. There are no forced sermons here — just authentic storytelling that breathes truth and hope.

Welcome to New York — Part 2 is already in progress and will be published independently — without agents, companies, or publishers — to ensure complete creative control and to protect the story’s spiritual essence from commercial dilution.
